ABOUT CAROL PARRIS

Carol hails from the City of Windsor, Ontario ,Canada. She attended the University of Windsor and was involved in forming many clubs and committees involving the African-Canadian students. She did many courses at the University in Arts and Education. Carol obtained her teaching certification and has worked in many daycare facilities. She is a well known and respected member of her community

Carol is a caregiver to her mother and as an Artist has helped people with their clothing choices and wardrobe. Carol is the mother of three grown children. Carol was always interested in Arts, Drawing, Sketching and Color Coordination

Before the start of the Covid-19 pandemic, her interest in being an artist was at the forefront for a new career and she has continued to do so on a daily basis since. She is looking forward to having her paintings in various galleries worldwide and has perfected her art and is improving everyday.

Art is something that she has always thought about but it has only now become a reality.

When we participate in Art, Paintings ,Drawing it can play an emotional game in our minds and we most times see a need to repress the feelings that have risen and we tend to want to get rid of the feelings any way we can. My art reflects a great deal of my early years of wanting and embracing freedom to see the sky and the space around me and to see myself as wanting to be free always to do the things I love and to embrace freedom ,love and nature. To adapt to the changes in life and that others will be able to see themselves in my growth and aspiration in doing the things that bring joy to their lives and the lives of others.

When you see my art and my collection , I am trusting that you will feel your best and will want to have one of my paintings hanging on your wall to inspire and direct you to make changes in your own lives to act and to do the things you love. No one can take action for you but you. You can seek help, professional help or you can seek your own help if you are also moving in a spiritual journey of growth.I am learning to align myself , my mind, my heart, my soul and my strength with my spirit. My art has helped me to do this and it gets easier everyday.

I am glad that I have started this journey now.

ACKNOWLEDGEMENTS

This collection took me three years to compile. It was inspirational and allowed me to grow in a way that I love myself and my achievements in my life even more. My family gave me the needed support to persevere and make things that appear overwhelming became easily manageable and doable. I learnt to put things, my emotions and my mind in places of joy and peace.

The action allowed me to heighten my senses allowing me to see colors and light in a different way.My children inspired me to make this group of paintings and think of myself and look into myself so that I can do the things that I love and bring this beautiful collection to fruition.For this, I thank all those who have helped me bring this collection to fruition.

INTRODUCTION

This collection contains over 60 paintings which will motivate you to purchase a painting, persevere in the face of insurmountable hardships. This collection will keep you upbeat when you are frustrated but most of all will spread love, joy and peace.

WHY THIS PAINTINGS COLLECTION

These paintings are a collection of life ,moving from one stage to another.This collection has taught that love is the most important thing in life. Love heals no matter what you are experiencing. The colors, the paint, the strokes of the paintbrush and so much more, all help to reduce the chaos, confusion, hurt, and despise to help you see life in one word : Love. Love transcends everything and I hope that everyone who sees the collection will see the hope and joy that I see.

HOW TO ENJOY MY COLLECTION /HOW TO LOOK AT EACH PAINTING

My paintings will enlighten you. It will allow you to enjoy my work. It should allow you to see the colors. It will allow you to see the brush in my hands and the paint on the paper. It will allow you to see an expression of the love in you; for you to believe that you can do what I have done.

SHARE MY COLLECTION WITH OTHERS

We are the light of the world, you and I .Some paintings,when you see them, will move you to share with your friends and families and on social media. I promise you that my paintings will get you to move and search your heart and minds to do the things you love and you will share your collections with others in the near future
